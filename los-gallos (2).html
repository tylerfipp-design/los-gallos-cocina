Our Story

Bienvenidos to Los Gallos

We came to Clawson with one mission: to bring real, authentic Mexican food to Metro Detroit. Every dish we make is rooted in tradition — fresh ingredients, bold salsas that pack real heat, and recipes passed down through generations.

From the moment you walk in, you're family. Our staff greets every guest with warmth, and our kitchen sends out every plate with pride. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or settling in for a feast, Los Gallos is your spot.
